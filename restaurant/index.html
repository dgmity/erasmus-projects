Menu

Khinkali

Classic Khinkali (Kalakuri)

Hand-crafted dumplings filled with a juicy blend of minced beef and pork, fresh cilantro, onions, and traditional Georgian spices.

Beef & Lamb Khinkali (Mtiuluri)

A rich and hearty mountain-style recipe featuring premium minced beef and lamb, seasoned with wild herbs and black pepper.

Cheese Khinkali (V)

A delicious vegetarian option loaded with a melted blend of traditional, salty Sulguni and Imeretian cheeses.

Khachapuri

Adjaruli Khachapuri (The Boat)

An open-faced, boat-shaped bread filled with a pool of melted Sulguni cheese, topped with a raw egg yolk and a pat of butter. Mix it well before eating!

Imeruli Khachapuri

A classic, circular Georgian flatbread stuffed to the brim with a gooey, savory blend of Imeretian and Sulguni cheeses.

Megruli Khachapuri

For true cheese lovers: a traditional Imeruli flatbread, but topped with an extra layer of melted, golden Sulguni cheese on the outside.

About Us

Buranok is a family-owned Georgian restaurant where centuries-old traditions meet modern hospitality. We bring the true spirit of Tbilisi to your table with authentic family recipes, hand-rolled khinkali, and legendary Georgian wines. Every guest here is treated like family—welcome to our feast!
